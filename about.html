About Me

Hello, I'm Hanna Khan. A photographer based in Miami, Florida.

I've been photographing for a few years now and have really enjoyed expressing my creative side through my photographs. I genuinely enjoy photographing many subjects, so I don't specialize in just one area (The more the merrier!). Apart from photography, some of my hobbies include running, traveling, and eating as many chocolate chip cookies as possible. I am available for any type of photography sessions, as well as shooting events in the Miami area. Prints of my personal work are also available. I'd love to hear from you, so feel free to contact me. Thank you for taking time to look at my site and work; I hope to meet you in the near future.
